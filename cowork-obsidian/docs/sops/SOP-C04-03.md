# Lesson 03 — Maintenance and Growing

This is your cheat-sheet lesson. Print it. Keep it next to your keyboard. Come back to it when something goes wrong.

---

## 1. The 5 operations cheat-sheet

These are the only commands you need to run your second brain.

| Command | What it does | When to run |
|---|---|---|
| `/second-brain build` | First-time setup of a wiki area | Once per area, ever |
| `/second-brain update` | Adds new raw notes to the wiki | Weekly — Sunday 5pm |
| `/second-brain health-check` | Finds problems in the wiki | Monthly — first Saturday |
| `/second-brain fold-back` | Folds outputs back into the wiki | Quarterly — first Sunday of Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct |
| `/second-brain refresh-hot` | Rewrites the small `hot.md` cache | Monthly — same day as health-check |

**Build runs only once. Update runs forever.**

## 2. Common problems and quick fixes

Most issues are easy. Here are the ones you will hit.

**Problem:** Claude says it can't find my vault.
**Fix:** Cloud sync is the cause 95% of the time. See lesson 01. Move the vault to a local folder.

**Problem:** I ran BUILD twice and now my wiki is full of duplicates.
**Fix:** Don't run BUILD twice. Use `/second-brain update` to add new raw notes. To clean the duplicates, ask Claude to merge them.

**Problem:** My wiki feels stale.
**Fix:** Run `/second-brain refresh-hot` to update the cache. If that doesn't help, run `/second-brain update`.

**Problem:** Claude is answering with old information.
**Fix:** Your wiki is behind your raw notes. Run `/second-brain update`.

**Problem:** I edited the wiki by hand and broke something.
**Fix:** Move your edit to a raw note. Run UPDATE. Claude will redo the wiki page properly.

**Problem:** How do I see what changed in the last health-check?
**Fix:** Look at the file `wiki/_health-check-<date>.md`. The wizard saves one each time.

**Problem:** I have more than 4 life areas and Claude is confused.
**Fix:** Pick your top 4 and merge or archive the rest. Claude works best with a small number of clear areas.

**Problem:** The wizard stopped halfway and I don't know where I am.
**Fix:** Run `/onboard-second-brain` again. The wizard saves your progress. It will pick up where you stopped.

## 3. Growing the system

When the basics feel boring, you're ready for more. Run `/level-up` in cowork-ai-os. It will suggest one of these next:

- **`/wiki-query`** — faster questions of your wiki, with quick / standard / deep modes.
- **`/save`** — capture from anywhere. Paste a transcript, drop a screenshot, paste a tweet. It lands in raw with a tag.
- **`/weekly-review`** — once a week, Claude pulls the lessons out of your week and adds them to the wiki.

Don't install all three at once. Pick one. Live with it for a month. Then add the next.

## 4. One-page reference card

**Print this page. Tape it next to your monitor.**

### Daily
- Drop notes in `raw/`. Anything you might want to find again.

### Weekly (Sunday 5pm)
- `/second-brain update`

### Monthly (First Saturday)
- `/second-brain health-check`
- `/second-brain refresh-hot`

### Quarterly (First Sunday of Jan / Apr / Jul / Oct)
- `/second-brain fold-back`

### When you ask the vault questions
- `/open-vault`
- Then ask Claude anything.

### Safe vault paths
- Mac / Linux: `~/SecondBrain/`
- Windows: `C:\Users\<your-name>\SecondBrain\`

### Three things to never do
- Don't edit the wiki by hand.
- Don't bulk-import old chats.
- Don't put the vault in iCloud, OneDrive, Dropbox, or Google Drive.
